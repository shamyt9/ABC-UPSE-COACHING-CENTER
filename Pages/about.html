Our Vision

To be the most trusted institution for holistic civil services preparation, nurturing future administrators who will lead India's growth story.

At ABC IAS Academy, we envision creating a learning ecosystem that goes beyond conventional coaching. Our focus is on developing analytical thinkers, ethical leaders, and compassionate administrators who can navigate the complexities of governance with wisdom and integrity.

We believe that UPSC preparation is not just about clearing an examination but about shaping minds capable of contributing meaningfully to nation-building. This philosophy guides our teaching methodology, curriculum design, and student mentorship approach.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, integrated preparation that empowers aspirants to excel at every stage of the UPSC examination through personalized mentorship and innovative pedagogy.

Teaching Philosophy

Conceptual clarity before content coverage. We emphasize understanding over memorization, application over theory, and critical thinking over rote learning.

Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Empathy, and Innovation. These values guide our interactions with students and shape our institutional culture.

Our 14-Year Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in UPSC preparation

2010

The Foundation

ABC IAS Academy was established in Mukherjee Nagar, Delhi with a single classroom and a vision to revolutionize UPSC preparation through integrated teaching.

2014

First Major Success

Our student secured AIR 34, marking the beginning of consistent results. Expanded to 3 centers across Delhi with specialized faculty for each subject.

2018

Digital Transformation

Launched online learning platform, making quality education accessible nationwide. Introduced hybrid learning model combining offline and online advantages.

2023

Milestone Achieved

Crossed 250+ selections mark with 15 students in top 100 ranks. Recognized as one of Delhi's premier institutes for comprehensive UPSC preparation.
